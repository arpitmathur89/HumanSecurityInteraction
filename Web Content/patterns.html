Patterns

Another wonder of the human brain. While we use it in most aspects of life, many of us might not be aware of them. Patterns are encapsulations of complex concepts, helping most of us to avoid the analytical approach of understanding the reasons, especially if the reasons were complex, or even unknown. Therefore, a pattern looks into a tried, tested, and true concept and simply encapsulates it for reuse. Once proven to have worked successfully, we don’t necessarily need to look into the reasons, but rather to find accurate ways to reuse it properly…more
